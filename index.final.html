Volleyball is one of my many passions. I have played, coached, and officiated the sport. Here is some useful information for everything you need to know about the heaven sent sport!



Here are some of the most imporant parts of the game. They are ranked in order of importance.

  1. Serving
    • Serving is by far one of the most important parts of the game.
    • If your team cannot serve, it will not be possible to win the game.
    • There are a couple of different types of serves, but if you can nail the standing float serve, it will prove to be very effective.



  2. Rotations
    • Rotations can be a difficult part of the game to understand.
    • They balance the game so the opponent does not have there best hitter in the front row for the entire game.
    • From personal experience, once the rotations are learned, they make the game more fun.
    • This is a normal layout of positions on the court. Players need to know there "home base" in order to move onto harder rotations.



  3. Approach
    • The approach is essential to be able to hit the ball with true force.
    • It can also be a difficult skill to master.
    • Most right handed people use the approach, right, left, right, left. With the last two steps sped up.
